Adelaide's younger prostate cancer support group

Fighters supporting other fighters

We are a group of guys in our 40s and 50s who are fighting prostate cancer. We created this group to help support other younger men who have unique challenges with being diagnosed at such a young age.

Keeping in touch

We have set up a few options to contact us and keep in touch with other members.

The Facebook and Whatsapp chat groups are private so the first time you go there, you'll be asked to join up. Anything you post will only be seen by other members of this group.

  • Our private Facebook group is where will will post any updates and upcoming events, such as meetings. It also allows members to chat to each other.

  • Our private Whatsapp chat is a quick and easy way to chat to other members.

  • Alternatively, if you have any questions or would like to ask the admin team something you can just email us.
